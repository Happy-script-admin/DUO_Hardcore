Guide

Place 2 Cowboys lvl 2 → Place 1 Electro lvl 2
Get 6 Cowboys lvl 2 (in total)
Upgrade 2 Cowboys to lvl 4 (one by one)
Place DJ lvl 3 (Green Track - Spam Ability)
Upgrade 4 Cowboys to lvl 4 → Get 10 Maxed Cowboys
Max DJ → Get 4 Electros lvl 4
Get 7 Max Rangers → Max Electros (set Strongest) → Spam Maxed 5th Slot
Wave 50: set DJ to red
Place 3 Farms lvl 1 → Upgrade 1 Farm to lvl 2
Place 2 Military Bases lvl 2
Get 8 Farms lvl 2
Upgrade 1 Military Base to lvl 4
Get 8 Farms lvl 3
Place 1 Commander lvl 2 (Use Ability constantly)
Get 5 Military Bases lvl 4 (one by one)
Place 7 Rangers lvl 2
Max Military Bases → Place 3 Commanders lvl 3 (Start Chaining)
Max Rangers → Get 4 Maxed Commanders → Spam Maxed 5th Slot
